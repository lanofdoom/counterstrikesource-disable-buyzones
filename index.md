## Installation
Extract ``lan_of_doom_disable_buyzones.tar.gz`` to your server's ``css/cstrike``
directory.

## Console Variables
``sm_lanofdoom_buyzones_disabled`` If ``1``, buyzones are disabled. ``1`` by
default.
